Summary

I've also driven company-wide adoption of AI agentic programming there — the only engineer championing it in my group, with influence reaching across the organization, contributing to a roughly 10x productivity increase for the teams that adopted it. I didn't pick up AI tools last year — I've been building and teaching agentic workflows to a technical audience for years.

HiveLink / Ash-Mesh

Swarm-robotics security architecture
  • Self-healing mesh network for swarm robotics on ESP32-class hardware, LoRa, and MQTT — designed to detect and quarantine compromised drones without human intervention.
  • Formal PRD, TRD, and Hardware BOM / Comms Trade Study documentation.
  • Quarantine state machine with 7 states (UNKNOWN, TRUSTED, SUSPECT, QUARANTINED, HONEYPOT, BLOCKED, RELEASED), tunable suspicion scoring, and recovery flows keyed to hardware root-of-trust.
  • Federated trust-list format, interop beacon format, and a decoy-data generator using canary tokens. Architecture is explicitly defensive by design — offensive/reflashing capability was deliberately declined.
